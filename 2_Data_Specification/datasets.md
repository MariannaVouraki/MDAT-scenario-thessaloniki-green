# Data Specification

## Dataset 1: Urban Tree Categories
- **Title**: Tree Categories
- **Description**: The dataset includes detailed information on urban forestry in the Municipality of Thessaloniki, providing valuable insights for the management and monitoring of trees across municipal communities. The data originate from an integrated project for the creation of a model system for urban green management and the cityâ€™s adaptation to climate change.
Dataset contents: Information on trees by municipal community (A, B, C, D). Data on the condition of trees by district. Categorization of trees based on species and characteristics. Statistical data on parks and public spaces with trees.
- **Source**: (TDS) Thessaloniki Data Space [https://tds.okfn.gr/dataset/37]
- **Format**: Excel (.xlsx)
- **Key Fields**: greek_name, scientific_name, total

## Dataset 2: Population Census
- **Title**: Permanent Population of the Municipality of Thessaloniki (2021)
- **Description**:Results of the 2021 Population and Housing Census concerning the permanent population of the Municipality of Thessaloniki. The data were collected by ELSTAT (Hellenic Statistical Authority) during the year 2021.
- **Source**: (TDS) Thessaloniki Data Space [https://tds.okfn.gr/dataset/207] 
- **Format**: Excel (.xlsx)
- **Key Fields**: Description, Permanent population
